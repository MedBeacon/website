The Sepsis Threat

A crisis hiding
in plain sight

Sepsis is a leading cause of hospital deaths, readmissions, and costs. For hospitals, managing it is a never-ending struggle.

350K+ Deaths each year in the U.S.
1 in 3 Hospital deaths involve sepsis
1.7M+ People diagnosed each year
$62B Annual treatment costs

Source: CDC, Sepsis Alliance

Our Solution

Why early detection
changes everything

Sepsis is a race against the clock — survival rates drop with every hour of delayed treatment. Yet most EHR-generated alerts lack specificity; studies suggest up to 90% are false positives or unnecessary. Too many alerts. Too little confidence. Too much risk.

Nurses

Need quicker, comprehensive screening that doesn't trigger unnecessary alerts to physicians.

Physicians

Need precise, patient-specific data with the ability to easily order sepsis bundles — even remotely.

Administrators

Need dashboards to monitor alert activity, false-positive rates, and protocol compliance across departments.

MedBeacon Platform

Designed to help,
not disrupt

MedBeacon's AI-guided system acts as a guardian assistant at the point of care. It combats alert fatigue by balancing objective clinical data with subjective observations — delivering timely, actionable insights that give clinicians flexibility in determining next steps.

Faster Detection

AI-driven mobile alerts with higher specificity, individualized to each patient, helping clinicians respond up to an hour sooner.

Guided Interventions

One-tap best-practice order sets and seamless EHR integration to accelerate care and preserve data integrity.

Continuous Monitoring

Automated, intelligent tracking ensures patients keep improving and alerts care teams instantly if recovery stalls.

MedBeacon uses trusted, enterprise-grade AI and Red Rover Health's real-time bidirectional EHR integration. Built by the team behind the St. John Sepsis Agent for Cerner (now Oracle), MedBeacon brings more than 20 years of clinical workflow innovation to healthcare's biggest challenges.

Sepsis Alerting

Order Tracking

Consolidated view of active, pending, and completed orders with status timers, reminders, results, and configurable completion notifications.

AI Chat

Secure interaction with the clinical record via AI — request patient summaries, protocol guidance, actionable recommendations, or medication queries.

Patient Lists & Summaries

Sepsis Risk Indicator

Continuously updated risk score derived from vital signs, labs, and clinical history — viewable on patient list views and detail screens.

Clinical Surveillance
